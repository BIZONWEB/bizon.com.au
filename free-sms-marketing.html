QtSMS

The beauty of QtSMS is the way collecting your customers' contact numbers. It automatically extracts contact information from your SMS inbox.



Get SMS from Customers

How to get SMS from your customers? It is easy. You can write down your mobile number on your flyers or posters with a phrase like "Text me PROPERTY, then we will send you our list of our latest properties.".



Build customer database

Your customers will send you SMS message with the keyword you selected, in this case, PROPERTY. All the message with your special keyword can be collected and added to your customer contact list on QtSMS mobile app.



Opt out easy

As you know Australian government prohibits unsolicited SPAM to be sent. That is the way we built in "Opt-out feature" in the QtSMS App. If your customer replies back with phrase "STOP" then this App will opt out the recipient automatically.
