If you google "business email hosting", you can find some companies saying they provides it for free or for less than $3 per month. But keep in mind, they bundled domain hosting and email hosting, so you have to buy both together. In case you want to buy email hosting seperately, you need to pay about $5 per user / month. Zoho email on the other hands, gives you free account with 5GB of storage. They allow you to upgrade your account when you need additional storage or additional account.
