Domain name

Your domain name is your website address on the internet. It gives you an online identity of your business. Company domain names come with extensions such as .com or .com.au. Domain names in Australia such as .com.au, .net.au and .org.au may only be registered to Australian sole traders, businesses or companies. Domain name is a limited resource, so many domain names are already occupied by someone else. Therefore, you need to carefully decide your domain name and search for the availability.
