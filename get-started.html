Your business

It is obvious that any website should be built for its' own business purpose. So your business type is a perfect starting point. What type of business are you running?



Company logos are intended to be the "face" of a company: They are graphical displays of a company's unique identity, and through colors and fonts and images they provide essential information about a company. Domain name

Your domain name is your website address on the internet. It gives you an online identity of your business. Company domain names come with extensions such as .com or .com.au. Domain names in Australia such as .com.au, .net.au and .org.au may only be registered to Australian sole traders, businesses or companies. Domain name is a limited resource, so many domain names are already occupied by someone else. Therefore, you need to carefully decide your domain name and search for the availability.



Email account

In business, it is important to maintain a professional image for keeping your customers’ trust and appearing credibility to them. When potential or existing customers contact you with questions, using an email address like sales@yourdomainname.com.au definitely looks professional than johnsmith1224@yahoo.com or sallykim1990@gmail.com. Many internet companies out there, provide decent email services at reasonable prices. Also, there is free email account service for starters.

Hosting

When your website is ready, it needs to be hosted somewhere on the internet, so that people can browse it. After you put your website on a hosting service, your domain name can be linked to your website. Then your website is finally done. In other words, your website is published.

There are many website hosting companies and services you can choose from. When you google "web hosting" you can find many hosting companies such as HOSTPAPA, GoDaddy, or CrazyDomains. They are cheap and some of them bundle domain name, email, hosting and templates. They also provide content management console, so you can maintain your website yourself. In order to use one of these services, however, you need to design a website yourself or ask website designer to build one for you. Also, you need to have enough knowledge to maintain your website on their system. Some of the services such as Wix, Weebly provide a DIY website, which allows you to save money by designing a website yourself.

Another issue is the server location. Most of these services are located outside of Australia. That means your website can be slow down a lot. Some servers are in Singapore, US or Ireland. Sometimes they are 5 times slower than servers in Australia.



SEO

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. By definition, SEO is a methodology of strategies, techniques, and tactics used to increase the number of visitors to your website by obtaining a high-ranking placement in the search results page of a search engine including Google, Bing, and Yahoo. The keyword is the one of a factor but there are much more things to consider in this competitive digital era.
